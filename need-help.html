Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, go to the Courses page, select the course you’re interested in, and click 'Enroll'. You may need to sign in first if you haven't already.

How do I access my enrolled courses?

Once enrolled, you can access your courses from the Dashboard. Click on the 'My Courses' section to view all the courses you’re enrolled in.

How do I reset my password?

Click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page, and follow the instructions. You'll receive an email with a link to reset your password.

How do I manage my profile settings?

To manage your profile, click on your profile icon at the top right and select 'Profile Settings'. Here you can update your personal information, change your password, and manage notifications.
